About My Journey

I hold both bachelor's and master's degrees in Art History and Science, and my career has been deeply rooted in the cultural sector.
Most recently, I was employed at the Berlin Biennale for Contemporary Art, where my responsibilities encompassed organizing opening days, events, and the public program.
However, a desire for a fresh start had been brewing within me, and when my son was born two years ago, I felt the time was right to make a change.

My journey into the world of web development began with an introductory course on Udacity. It was during this course that I discovered a profound passion, ambition, and a burning desire to embrace a new field.
In November 2023, I successfully completed the Full Stack Web Development Program at CareerFoundry. During my journey at CareerFoundry, my primary focus shifted to JavaScript, and I also gained proficiency in TypeScript.

My heart lies in front-end development, with a particular emphasis on React and React Native. But, of course, I'm wholeheartedly open to new challenges and experiences.
